What is SustainStitch

Definition

SustainStitch is a website that provides information about sustainable materials. It provides information about the different types of sustainable materials, and whether your clothes are of that particular sustainable material. It also provides places to donate to help the cause.

Causes

Our cause is to help the environment by reducing the amount of material waste that is generated. We want to make sure that we spread awareness about the sustainability of our clothes.

Impact

Our impact is to end up with a more sustainable world. We want to make sure that we limit fast fashion and product waste as much as possible. We hope that this website will help people to make a change in people's lifestyle choices.

Addressing

This website was made to bring awareness to the harsh impact that fast fashion has on the environment, community, and on people. Fast fashion has been a long-going issue that has nobody really talks about or makes an active effort to fix in comparison to other issues. We hope that this website will not only bring awareness to the issue, but allow people to realize the seriousness of this issue and make an active effort to become more sustainable.
